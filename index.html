Marching Toward Success

Welcome to Kurunduwatte Royal College,

Join us on a journey of learning and growth. Explore our site to discover how we inspire and support our students to achieve their best.

Discover Our School

Kurunduwatte Royal College, located in the heart of Gampola, Central Province. A thriving educational institution dedicated to shaping the leaders of tomorrow. Since our establishment on the 26th of March, 2009, we have been committed to providing an exceptional learning experience to students from grades 6 to 13. Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where every student can excel academically, grow personally, and prepare for a successful future.

About Us

Our school is a vibrant community where students are encouraged to explore their full potential. Guided by the motto "Best among the best!", we strive for excellence in everything we do. From a rigorous academic curriculum to a wide range of extracurricular activities, Kurunduwatte Royal College offers a well-rounded education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to succeed.

Why Choose Us

  1. Experienced Leadership: Our school has flourished, consistently achieving high academic standards, thanks to the dedicated efforts of our experienced leadership team and committed staff.
  2. Holistic Development: We focus on the overall development of our students, emphasizing both academic success and personal growth.
  3. Comprehensive Sports Facilities: Our school boasts a variety of sports facilities, including a swimming pool and areas for many sports such as cricket, football, badminton, and athletics. These amenities help our students stay active, develop teamwork skills, and pursue their athletic passions.
  4. Hostel Facilities: For students who come from distant areas, we offer comfortable hostel facilities that provide a safe and supportive living environment. Our hostel is equipped with modern amenities to ensure that students have a home away from home.
  5. Diverse and Inclusive Community: Kurunduwatte Royal College is proud to be a multicultural and inclusive institution. We welcome students from Sinhala, Tamil, Muslim, and Christian backgrounds, creating a rich and diverse community where everyone is respected and valued.

Events

Kurunduwatte Royal College hosts diverse events year-round, including cultural festivals, sports competitions, and academic exhibitions. These activities foster school spirit, showcase student talents, and build a strong, inclusive community.
